To You — Wherever You Are

I don't know where you are right now, or what you're doing — but somehow, I feel connected to you in ways I can't explain.

Maybe we haven't met yet. Maybe we've crossed paths without knowing. Or maybe the universe is still preparing us — polishing us gently into the people we need to become for each other.

I imagine you sometimes — your smile, your voice, the quiet way you'll look at me like I matter. Like I'm home.

And I want you to know this: I am waiting for you, not in sadness, but in the most beautiful kind of hope.

I'm growing, becoming better, learning to love deeply — so that when you come into my life, I can give you something real. Something rare. Something that took years to become.

If you ever feel lost, just know that somewhere, someone is thinking about you, believing in you, and already choosing you — even before meeting you.

Maybe destiny is slow, but I trust it completely. Because if it leads me to you, then every single wait will be worth it.

yours, in time & in trust — Madhu 🌸
